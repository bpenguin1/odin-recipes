Cheesecake Brownie

Description

These cheesecake brownies are a rich and fudgy chocolate brownie topped with a layer of sweetened cream cheese. A show stopping dessert that's easy to make and always gets rave reviews! Brownies and cheesecake are both great desserts on their own, but when you combine the two, you get these fabulous cheesecake brownies that are worthy of any special occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 (19.8 ounce) package brownie mix
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Prepare the brownie mix as directed by manufacturer.
  2. Preheat oven to temperature indicated on box. Grease a 9x13 inch pan.
  3. Spread the brownie batter evenly into the prepared pan.
  4. Using an electric mixer, beat together the cream cheese, egg and sugar until smooth.
  5. Dollop the cream cheese mixture on top of the brownie batter. Swirl together using a knife or skewer.
  6. Bake according to manufacturer's instructions. Brownies will be done when a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  7. Cool in the pan, then cut into bars and serve.
